Soup Joumou

Haitian Soup Joumou

Haitian soup joumou is widely eaten on january first every year to commemorate Haitian independeence day.

ingredients

  • The squash
  • combined meat-Chiken, goat, porc
  • peeling the vegetables-cabbage, malanga, scallions or green onion, peppers, carrots, potatoes
  • macaroni/spagethi
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. cut the meat into cubes-marinated the meats
  2. add the Haitian seasoning
  3. Add garlic and remaining species
  4. mix well and marinated
  5. Add the squash liquid to the blender
  6. cut the vegetables
