Goat and platain banana fried

Tasso Kabrit, Bannan peze ak plikliz

Haitian fried goat meat with plantain and pikliz

Haitian soup joumou is widely eaten on january first every year to commemorate Haitian independeence day.

ingredients

  • Plaintain
  • Goat meat
  • Pikliz-cabages, carrots, great and red peper, garlic, vinegar|malanga
  • Onions
  • Sweet peper

Steps

  1. Diagonaly Cut the plaintain after peeling it
  2. Transfer the cut plainttain into hot oil until cook
  3. Press the cook plaintain pieces
  4. Dip the pressed plaintain into salt warm water
  5. Return them into hot oil and for 3 to five mn maximum
  6. Make the Pikliz with cabbage, carrots, scotch bonnet peppers, garlic, vinegar and lime or lemon juice.
  7. Wash the meat then spray vinager unto it then put it into a boiled water
  8. Apply the seasoning- Haitian seasoning.
  9. Boil and cook the meat
  10. Fry the meat
  11. Add the fry meat with pikliz and plaintain
